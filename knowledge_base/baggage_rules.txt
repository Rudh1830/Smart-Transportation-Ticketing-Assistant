BAGGAGE AND LUGGAGE RULES (SIMPLIFIED)

Flights:
- Cabin baggage usually allowed up to 7 kg.
- Check-in baggage allowed from 15 kg to 25 kg based on airline and ticket type.
- Sharp objects, flammable items, and liquids above 100 ml are restricted in cabin bags.

Trains and Buses:
- There is usually no strict weight limit, but extremely heavy or large items may require special permission.
- Keep valuables and important documents in a small bag that is always with you.

Cabs and Bike Taxis:
- Carry only small backpacks or cabin-sized luggage in bike taxis.
- In cabs, check boot space before starting the trip.
