TRAVEL SAFETY GUIDELINES

1. Prefer official booking apps and websites instead of random agents.
2. Verify vehicle number and driver details before boarding a cab or bike taxi.
3. Avoid sharing personal financial details like OTP or card PIN with anyone.
4. At night, sit in well-lit and populated areas while waiting for your transport.
5. For train travel, lock your luggage with a chain if sleeping in an overnight journey.
6. Always wear seat belts in cars and helmets on bikes.
