GENERAL TRAVEL TIPS

1. Always keep a digital and physical copy of your ticket and ID.
2. Reach the boarding point at least 30 minutes early for bus and train, and 2 hours early for domestic flights.
3. Carry a small power bank, water bottle, and basic medicines.
4. Check traffic conditions before leaving for the station or airport.
5. For night travel, share your live location with a trusted contact.
